Library Card

Getting Your Card

Visit the library with your student ID to get your library card. The card is valid for your entire academic stay.

Card Usage

  • Required for borrowing books and materials
  • Access to electronic resources
  • Entry to restricted library areas

Borrowing Guidelines

Loan Periods

  • General Books: 14 days
  • Reserved Books: 3 days
  • Magazines: 7 days
  • Multimedia: 3 days

Renewals

Books can be renewed twice if no one has reserved them. Renewal can be done online or at the circulation desk.

Facilities

Study Areas

  • Individual study carrels
  • Group study rooms
  • Silent reading areas

Computer Labs

  • Internet access
  • Printing services
  • Scanning facilities

Rules and Regulations

General Rules

  • Maintain silence in designated areas
  • No food and drinks in the library
  • Handle library materials with care
  • Follow the dress code

Penalties

  • Late returns: Fine per day
  • Damaged materials: Replacement cost
  • Lost items: Full cost plus processing fee
