Privacy Policy

Introduction

Pan Pacific University Library is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information in accordance with data protection laws and regulations.

Last updated: January 2025

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name and contact details
  • Student/Faculty ID number
  • Email address
  • Department/Course information
  • Library card details

Usage Information

  • Borrowing history
  • Online resource access logs
  • Search queries
  • Computer and facility usage

How We Use Your Information

Library Services

  • Managing library accounts
  • Processing loans and returns
  • Sending notifications and reminders
  • Providing personalized services

Service Improvement

  • Analyzing usage patterns
  • Improving resource allocation
  • Enhancing user experience
  • Service evaluation and planning

Data Protection

Security Measures

  • Encrypted data storage
  • Secure access controls
  • Regular security audits
  • Staff training on data protection

Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to provide library services and comply with legal requirements. Records are securely deleted when no longer needed.

Your Rights

User Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data corrections
  • Object to data processing
  • Request data deletion

To exercise your rights or ask questions about our privacy practices, please contact the Library Privacy Officer.
