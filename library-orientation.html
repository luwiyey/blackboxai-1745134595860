Library Orientation

Orientation Overview

Our library orientation program introduces new students and faculty to the library's resources, services, and facilities. Learn how to make the most of our collections and digital resources through guided tours and hands-on workshops.

Orientations are available in both group and individual formats.

Available Sessions

Group Orientation

  • 90-minute session
  • Up to 30 participants
  • General overview
  • Basic database training

Individual Orientation

  • 60-minute session
  • Personalized guidance
  • Subject-specific resources
  • Advanced search techniques
