Borrowing Guidelines

Eligibility

Who Can Borrow

  • Current students with valid ID
  • Faculty and staff members
  • Research scholars
  • Alumni with special permissions

Required Documents

  • Valid university ID card
  • Current semester registration
  • Library card

Loan Periods

Undergraduate Students

  • Books: 14 days
  • Magazines: 7 days
  • Media: 3 days
  • Maximum Items: 5

Graduate Students

  • Books: 30 days
  • Magazines: 14 days
  • Media: 7 days
  • Maximum Items: 8

Renewals and Returns

Renewal Policy

  • Items can be renewed twice if not reserved
  • Renewal must be done before due date
  • Online renewal available through library portal
  • Special materials may have different renewal policies

Returns

  • Return items at the circulation desk
  • After-hours return box available
  • Items must be in good condition

Fines and Penalties

Late Returns

  • Books: ₱10 per day
  • Reserved Materials: ₱20 per day
  • Media Items: ₱15 per day

Lost or Damaged Items

  • Replacement cost of the item
  • Additional processing fee of ₱200
  • Temporary suspension of borrowing privileges

Accumulated fines may result in the suspension of library privileges until settled.
