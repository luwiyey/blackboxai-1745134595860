Our Mission

Mission Statement

The Pan Pacific University Library is committed to supporting the university's academic mission by providing comprehensive resources, innovative services, and expertise to foster research, learning, and discovery within our diverse community.

"Empowering minds, enabling discovery, enriching futures."

Accessibility

Ensuring equitable access to resources and services for all
