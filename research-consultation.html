Research Consultation

Service Overview

Our research consultation service provides one-on-one assistance with your research needs. Meet with a subject specialist librarian who can help you develop research strategies, find relevant resources, and navigate our databases effectively.

Consultations are available both in-person and online via video conference.

Areas of Assistance

Literature Review

Help with conducting comprehensive literature searches and reviews

Database Navigation

Guidance on using academic databases and search strategies

Citation Help

Assistance with citation styles and reference management tools

Research Methods

Support with research methodology and data collection

Schedule a Consultation

What to Expect

Before the Consultation

  • Confirmation email with meeting details
  • Pre-consultation questionnaire
  • Resource recommendations

During the Consultation

  • One-hour focused discussion
  • Hands-on database training
  • Personalized research strategies

After the Consultation

  • Summary of discussion points
  • Follow-up resources
  • Additional consultation if needed
